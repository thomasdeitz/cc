About the Band

Candy Cigarette is an original blues-rock band made up of Lucas - vocals / guitar / piano & his twin Isaac - vocals / bass. These two prove rock & roll is far from dead.

After cutting their teeth on piano at age 6, the Deitz brothers picked up something a little louder and grittier in the spring of 2019. It didn’t take long for them to translate what they were learning into original songs, so in the fall of 2019 Candy Cigarette was born as a way to focus their creativity and give it direction.

The duo recently released their self-titled debut EP, available to stream pretty much everywhere now. They are always looking for opportunities to share their music with others, performing when ever they get the chance.
